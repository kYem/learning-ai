## Where to Start

**If you want to be job-ready fastest:** Start with `roles/ai-application-engineer/learning-path.md`. Your TypeScript + backend experience maps directly. ~52 hours of focused work.

**If you want the highest long-term ceiling:** Start with `roles/ml-platform-engineer/learning-path.md`. Your K8s + infra experience is the foundation. ~75 hours.

**For either path**, the `reference/learning-ladder.md` gives you the full conceptual progression, and `reference/resources/curated-resources.md` tells you exactly what to study and in what order.
